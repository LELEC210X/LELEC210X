For this hands-on session, you will need:
\begin{itemize}
    \item A computer with GNU Radio installed
    \item 1 LimeSDR Mini board
    \item 1 Nucleo board with a S2LP radio
    \item 2 SMA antennas and 1 SMA cable
\end{itemize}
